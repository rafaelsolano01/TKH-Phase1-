QUESTION 2: Why must a SOC analyst NEVER use "Bridged" mode when detonating unknown malware?
ANSWER: The SOC analyst must never use "bridged" mode becauyse it attaches the virtual  machine directly to the physical local network (LAN) as if it were a  real device. THis allows malware to bypass  the host's isolation and move laterally to attack other devices on the network, such as the host machine,  routers, or other connected hardware, potentially leading to a full network compromise.
